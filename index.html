Crimson Desert World Map — All 5 armor pieces are located in the western side of Demeniss 📍 Western Demeniss

The Frostcursed Armor Set is essential for surviving the Spire of Frost dungeon and other ice-cold areas of Demeniss. Each piece provides Ice Resistance Lv 7, making it the best cold-weather gear in the game. Start in the northwest of Demeniss at Deadfire Mountain and work your way south.

Head to the north region of Demeniss, above the "D" in "Demeniss" on the map. Look for Chattering Rocks.
2
Locate the wall covered in green vines blocking the entrance. Use Blinding Flash (L1+R1+L1) to burn the vines away.
3
Push through into the newly revealed room. The Treasure Chest glows from the outside — it's yours.
Watch out for tough rock monsters guarding Chattering Rocks. Either avoid them by sneaking straight to the loot, or prepare for a fight.

1
Enter the Sanctum of Expiation. This is controlled by the Antumbra Order — clear them out first if you want an easier time.
2
Once inside, find the half wall with thorn roots covering the open bottom section. It's along the right wall, not too far from the main entrance stairs.
3
Position yourself in a corner, then use Blinding Light to burn the roots. Crouch and go through the small path — watch for spike traps on the floor.
4
Turn right, head back toward where you came from. The treasure chest is at the end. Warning: If you trigger the spike trap, you'll need to fast travel out.
The floor near the entrance crumbles when you stand on it. Move carefully, don't linger near the entrance stairs.

Go to Mistshard Cave in the far north of Demeniss, within the Smoking Lands. Head north of Deadfire Mountain to find the waterfall.
2
Enter through the upper right bank. Behind the waterfall is a wall that looks solid.
3
Use Mine or Force Palm on the center of the wall to open a golden passage.
4
Follow the passage down to the cave interior. The chest with your armor is inside, along with multiple Gold Ore nodes.
This is the highest defense piece of the set (+14 defense). Prioritize this if you're only going for one item. Mistshard Cave is the toughest location — bring healing items.

❄️ Complete Set Bonus
Ice Resistance Lv 7 + Chilling Death Immunity

With all 5 pieces equipped, you gain Ice Resistance Lv 7 — making the Spire of Frost dungeon and the frozen wastes of western Demeniss fully traversable without freezing to death. The set also protects against chilling death mechanics in high-altitude and deep-winter areas.
